Scramble egg

Egg are one of my fvorite foods. Here is a recipe for rich scramble egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 egg
  • 1lbs butter
  • 2lbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from edge of the part to the corner every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette
  5. When the egg are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until serve)
